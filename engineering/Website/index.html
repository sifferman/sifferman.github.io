Motivation

In summer 2020, I had just finished my most intense quarter yet (23 units) and COVID was forcing everyone indoors, so naturally I needed a large project to keep busy while maintaining my momentum from school. Thus, ethan.sifferman.dev was born.

This website stores a collection of my personal projects in recent years. I don’t always have time to keep it updated, but it was a fun summer project to keep my mind off the apocalypse.
